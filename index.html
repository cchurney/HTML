FAQ's

    What is Musicality?

    Musicality is a platform upon which users can review and rate songs. Users can browse other users' ratings and top charts. On your profile, you can keep track of your listening and organize songs into collections.

    Does it cost to use Musicality?

    No! Users can rate songs as well as view other users' ratings for free! However, by upgrading to a premium subscription, users can connect with friends and receive personalized song recommendations based off their ratings.

    Can I listen to music here?

    No, however we are collaborating with Spotify to make Musicality and Spotify a seamless experience while you are listening.

    Are my reviews and ratings public?

    Yes! All user content is visible on Musicality

    How do I find my friends?

    Musicality can access your contacts and suggest users based on their connected phone numbers. You can also add users by the search bar.
